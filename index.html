Weighted Estimate Calculator

Instructions

Think of the task you need to do. Then input your best guess for a low, mid, and high estimate.

Your estimate can be in hours, days, dollars, etc. Use whatever unit makes sense for the task.

How does it work?
The calculator takes a weighted average favoring your mid-range estimate.

The formula used by this calculator is: Weighted = (Low + (4*Mid) + High)/6

Uses

The calculator was created to assist with estimating hours for software development projects. Other possible uses:

  • Estimates for Hourly Work
  • Time Needed for School Project
  • Money Needed for an Event
  • Employees/Contractors Needed for a Job

Example

How long will it take, in hours, to build this calculator?

Low: 1

Mid: 2

High: 8

Weighted Estimate: 2.83 hours
